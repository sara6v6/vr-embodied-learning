Embodied condition

This condition uses camera-attached avatar hands, gaze-directed interaction, and teleportation as minimal body ownership and agency cues.

Use the HUD links to complete the post-survey or inspect local results.

Controls

The small circle at the center of the screen is the gaze cursor.

  • Drag mouse: look around
  • WASD keys: move precisely through the classroom
  • Center circle: gaze cursor
  • Hold the center circle on a labeled sphere for 2 seconds: open a knowledge card
  • Click floor: teleport quickly to that location
  • Use Post-survey when finished

How to explore this environment

The small circle at the center of the screen is the gaze cursor.

  1. Drag to look around.
  2. Use WASD keys for precise movement through the classroom.
  3. Hold the center circle on a labeled sphere for 2 seconds to open a knowledge card.
  4. Click the floor for quick teleportation.
  5. When finished, use the Post-survey link.
