Study protocol

How to experience the prototype

  1. 1. Complete the pre-survey and select a condition.
  2. 2. Explore the selected virtual learning environment.
  3. 3. Return here and complete the post-survey.
  4. 4. View the local results dashboard and export anonymous JSON if useful.

Desktop interaction guide
  • Drag the mouse to look around.
  • In the embodied condition, move the center reticle onto a labeled sphere and hold still for 2 seconds to open a card.
  • Click the floor to teleport, then use the Post-survey link when finished.
  • Recommended browser: Chrome or Edge.
